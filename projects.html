ProjectSUB

This tool functions as a real time accessibility aid for live presentations.

The Problem: Non-native English speakers frequently struggle to fully grasp lectures due to pronunciation nuances and delivery speed, creating a significant communication barrier.

The Solution: Leveraging the utility of closed captions, this app generates real-time subtitles alongside the speaker’s visuals. This allows the audience to read along instantly, maximizing comprehension.

ProjectLINKS

An AI-powered notepad that integrates directly into the browser to manage ephemeral web content.

The Problem: Despite the abundance of note-taking apps, there is no streamlined way to manage "digital clutter." Users frequently encounter random links—like wishlists or niche tools—that do not warrant a permanent bookmark but still need to be saved.

The Solution: A "New Tab" page integration that acts as a centralized hub. Instead of manual sorting, the integrated AI analyzes and automatically categorizes loose links, ensuring an organized workspace with zero friction.
